‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find rugby games near me?
Use the "Join a Game" feature to browse upcoming matches. Games are sorted by distance from your location.
How do I create a new game?
Tap "Get a Game On" on the home screen, fill in the team names, location, date, and time, then submit your game.
How do I join a game?
Browse available games, tap on one you're interested in, and use the contact options to reach out to the game organizer.
Can I filter games by date?
Yes! You can filter by today, tomorrow, this week, next week, or select a custom date.
How do notifications work?
Enable push notifications to get alerts about new games, game updates, and messages from other players.
